How to Use:

1. Start the bot: Send `/start` to get a welcome message.
2. Use `/session <your_string>` to start a Telethon session.
   - It will begin listening to your messages and auto-save self-destruct media.
3. Use `/status` to check active sessions (for all users).
4. Use `/help` to view all available commands.
